Galactic Disharmony (2020)

Galactic Disharmony is a personal project I worked on in my spare time, designing and implementing all of the programming for the games features.

The gameplay focuses on a turn based solar system map where the player constructs their armies and fights for control of planets of which the battles are played out in a first-person shooter experience with their units.

SKIMMERZ (2017)

SKIMMERZ was the first game I developed. For this game as well as all of the programming for the gameplay I also created all of the Art assets and textures in Blender.

The gameplay focuses on singleplayer arena combat with vehicles called Skimmerz, winning consecutive matches would unlock additional Skimmerz with each having unique weapons and abilities.
