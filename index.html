Galactic Disharmony (2020)

Galactic Disharmony is a personal project I worked on in my spare time, programming and all of the gameplay features within Unity such as FPS mechanics, drivable ground and air vehicles, game AI and a turn based strategy component. Additionaly I created the majority of the art assets such as all of the vehicles, weapons and characters within Blender.

The gameplay focuses on a turn based solar system map where the player constructs their armies and fights for control of planets of which the battles are played out in a first-person shooter experience with their units.

Puzzle Box (2021)

My role while developing this game was implementing all of the networking. This was done using PhotonPUN in the Unity game engine.

The gameplay for Puzzle Box is centered around players taking turns to solve a puzzle on the box, while a player is solving a puzzle the other players are shown a clue for their puzzle. They must then communicate with them to complete the puzzle.

Duplipoint (2019)

Duplipoint was the first mobile game and puzzle game I created. For this project I used Paint.Net to create the graphics and Unity for the game itself. Additionally I used Unity Ads to implement in-game advertisements in the form of an optional video Ad to double the upgrade points a player gets.

Gameplay: Each turn the player has to select 5 coloured squares. Once they have selected 5 squares they can end their turn. Once the turn ends their selected squares will change colour and the squres of the most common colour will be removed from play. If the most common colour is equal to the target colour the player is given then they will score points. If all the squares have been removed they lose.
